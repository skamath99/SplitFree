SplitFree

Split group expenses on iOS — every feature free, no ads, no accounts, no servers.

All the code here was created by Claude Code. None of it was reviewed by a human.

SplitFree tracks shared expenses for trips, housemates, and friend groups. It splits bills fairly, keeps running balances, and simplifies debts down to the fewest possible payments. Features that other expense-splitting apps put behind a subscription are simply free here.

Features

  • Groups & expenses — organize costs by trip, house, or crew; unlimited everything.
  • Flexible splits — equal, exact amounts, percentages, or shares, with penny-exact math.
  • Itemization — split by line item, with tax and tip spread proportionally to what each person ordered.
  • Debt simplification — balances collapse to the fewest possible payments.
  • Settle up, your way — share a request through any app you like (Apple Cash in Messages, Venmo, anything). A payment is only recorded when you explicitly mark it as paid — SplitFree never moves or verifies money itself.
  • Receipt scanning — on-device OCR reads the line items straight into the itemized split, so each dish goes to whoever ordered it; the photo never leaves your phone.
  • Currencies — 30 currencies with built-in offline conversion.
  • Search & charts — full-text search across all groups; monthly and category spending breakdowns.
  • Recurring expenses — rent and subscriptions add themselves.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

SplitFree does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • All data stays on your device. Groups, members, expenses, and settlements are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party — there is no server.
  • No accounts. The app requires no sign-up, login, email address, or phone number.
  • No third-party services. No analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporters, no tracking of any kind.
  • Receipt photos are processed entirely on-device with Apple's Vision framework and are not stored or uploaded.
  • Your control. Deleting a group, expense, or the app itself permanently removes the associated data from your device.
  • If iCloud sync is added in a future version, data would sync only through your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy terms, never through our servers — we would still have no access to it.
